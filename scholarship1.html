Cambodia - Scholar Application 2022

COUNTRY
The United States
DEADLINE
October 15, 2021
CATEGORY
  • Bachelor Degree

SHE-CAN is looking for young Cambodian women interested in applying for our Leadership Program in 2022! Through the program, accepted scholars will apply for U.S. college and university scholarships, receive guidance and support from a team of U.S. mentors, and participate in leadership training. We're so glad that you are interested in becoming a SHE-CAN scholar, and we're looking forward to learning more about you through this online application. The application deadline is October 15, 2021.

Program eligibility:

  • Women between 17-20 years old
  • Passionate about changing Cambodia
  • Graduating secondary school before December 2021
  • Living within 1 hour of Phnom Penh for SAT prep classes, or can live near Phnom Penh during the class
  • From a low-income household
  • Interested in pursuing an undergraduate degree at a U.S. college or university (SHE-CAN does not help students seeking graduate degrees.)
  • Already leaders in their school or community
  • Strong English speakers: Your ability to speak and write in English is critical because all U.S. universities require a high score on the TOEFL or IELTS language exam. Therefore, if your English is not strong, we recommend that you spend a year studying and apply next year.
  • Academically strong: Consistently ranked high in your class with strong grades and strong National Exam score.
  • Dedicated to the program: Able to dedicate your time in January 2022 – June 2022 to complete the pre-college program. You must agree to not work at a full-time job during this period.
  • Able to access a strong internet connection and computer daily
